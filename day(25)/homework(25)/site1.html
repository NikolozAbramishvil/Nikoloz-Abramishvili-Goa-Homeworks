BMW-history

The BMW name stands for Bayerische Motoren Werke GmbH. BMW was created in 1917 from the Munich
firm Rapp-Motorenwerke. The company was incorporated into Knorr-Bremse AG in 1920 before being
refounded as BMW AG in 1922. It was the successor of Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG, founded in 1916
